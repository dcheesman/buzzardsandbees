October 25 - 26, 2019

Buzzards and Bees is Provo's first downtown multi-venue music and arts festival. Every October, the city will find itself haunted by the living. Local artists, musicians, and business goers show up to present their best spooky selves. During its first year, Buzzards and Bees hosted 60+ local bands in 10 venues. Attendees enjoyed secret shows, street performers, costume contests, spooky storytelling, movie viewings, and a Goth Prom kickoff party. All within 4 blocks of each other in Downtown Provo.
